OUR LOCATION

CANGGU

Our location is very unique. Situated at Echo Beach, Canggu – one of the best beaches for surfing in Bali known by surfers all over the world, rapidly gaining reputation of being the hippest village on Bali with all it’s quaint little eateries, charming boutiques and specialty shops all coexisting amongst green rice fields. Definitely a place to visit off the beaten path. Our salon is situated 45 minutes from the airport and just 15 minutes from the cosmopolitan heart of Bali, Seminyak.

Jalan Pantai Batu Mejan, Echo Beach, Canggu, Bali


SEMINYAK

Whether you are a Bali regular or just heard it through the grapevine, Seminyak is the snazzy hub of Bali where to head for a bit of glitz and glam. Upmarket and popular (without the notorious Kuta crowds), Seminyak is spilling with uber-cool cafes, hip restaurants, trendy boutiques and cool beach bars. HAIR is conveniently located across the road from the all famous award winning restaurant Mama San.

Jalan Kayu Aya 1A, Seminyak (Next to Sambal Shrimp)
